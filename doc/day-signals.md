## Day 05

### Digital signals

```
    | |__________| |
 ___|/|          |\|______
  1 |2|    3     |4|  5

1 = off  (also false, disabled, low, idle, unset, inactive)
2 = up   (also trigger, enable, raise)
3 = on   (also true, enabled, high, hold, set, active)
4 = down (also release, disable, drop)
5 = off
```
